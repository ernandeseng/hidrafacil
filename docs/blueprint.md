## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Comprehensive listing of hydraulic, electrical, and irrigation products with detailed specifications and high-quality images.
- Brands Showcase: Dedicated section to feature partner brands like Rain Bird, Amanco PEX GAS, and Nambei, highlighting their expertise and product offerings.
- Service Overview: Detailed overview of specialized services, including professional irrigation system design, hydraulic system consultation, and electrical installation guidance.
- Contact Form: A fully functional contact form for inquiries and support.
- Instant Quote: Generative AI tool that generates an estimated quote based on user-provided project details. Connects to product database to fetch the cost of each item and compute an approximate amount.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Professional blue (#1E3A8A) taken from the logo to inspire trust.
- Background color: Light gray (#F3F4F6), a desaturated version of the primary, providing a clean and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Vibrant orange (#F59E0B), also from the logo, to highlight key actions and important information.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a modern sans-serif, for clear and legible text throughout the site.
- Minimalist icons representing different product categories and services for quick visual navigation.
- Clean and spacious layout with generous use of white space to focus on content and improve readability.
- Subtle fade-in effects and smooth transitions to enhance user experience without being distracting.
